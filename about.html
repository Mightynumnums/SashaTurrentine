Sasha is interested in capturing what is human, what is different, what is fleeting. She loves the simplicity of food, the ritual of wedding, the intimacy of portrait, and the inspiration of travel. She shoots digital, 35mm and medium format film.
Sasha is currently based in Brooklyn, New York, and is available for collaboration and assignments near and far.
